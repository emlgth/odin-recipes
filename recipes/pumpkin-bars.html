These pumpkin bars look delicious!

These easy pumpkin bars are full of fall flavor with a moist texture and a decadent cream cheese frosting. So far, I haven't found anyone who doesn't love them!

Ingredients

For the Cake:

  • eggs
  • pumpkin puree
  • white sugar
  • vegetable oil
  • all-purpose flour
  • cinnamon
  • baking soda
  • baking powder
  • salt

For the Frosting:

  • butter
  • cream cheese
  • vanilla extract
  • confectioner's sugar

Steps

Baking the Cake

  1. Beat the wet ingredients in a bowl until well-combined
  2. Sift the dry ingredients together in a separate bowl, then add the dry mixture to the wet mixture
  3. Spread the batter in an ungreased jelly roll pan
  4. Bake in a preheated oven until the cake bounces back when gently pressed

Making the Frosting

  1. Beat the butter and cream cheese together until smooth, then stir in the vanilla
  2. Add the confectioners' sugar a little at a time, beating until mixture is smooth

Finishing Touches

  1. Evenly spread the frosting over the cooled cake
  2. Cut into 24 equal squares
